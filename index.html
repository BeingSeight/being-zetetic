— Three modes —

One syllabus. Three depths.

Each mode generates a different shape of learning document from the same syllabus. Pick the one your mind needs right now.

— 01 —

Zetetic Thread

A pure thought-map flow. Every concept arises naturally from the previous one. The reader is never told anything they could have asked — they are led to ask it themselves.

BEST FOR: deep first-time understanding
View Example
— 02 —

Zetetic Sift

An MCQ-based stream. One-liner questions with 4 options, then one-liner explanations of why each is right or wrong. Every wrong answer is plausible to a hasty reader.

BEST FOR: active recall and exam prep
View Example
— 03 —

Zetetic-Socratic Hybrid

Thread + Sift, phase by phase. Build a concept, test it, build the next. The full protocol — for when you need to understand something forever, not just today.

BEST FOR: complete mastery with retention

— How to use —

Four steps. Then you own it.

— STEP 01 —

Choose a mode

Thread for understanding, Sift for testing, Hybrid for both. Click "Copy Prompt".

— STEP 02 —

Add a syllabus

Paste the prompt into Claude, ChatGPT, or Gemini. Replace <PASTE YOUR SYLLABUS HERE> with your topic.

— STEP 03 —

AI generates

The model returns a complete learning document. Every question leads to the next. No skipping, no filler.

— STEP 04 —

Read once. Own forever.

Work through it. The protocol is designed so that the final checkpoint proves you got it.
